Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a donation?

+

Making a donation is simple! Visit our donation page, select your preferred amount, and complete the secure payment process. Your contribution will directly support families in need.

Is my donation secure and tax-deductible?

+

Yes! All donations are processed through secure payment gateways with SSL encryption. We are a registered nonprofit organization, and your donation is tax-deductible. You'll receive a receipt for tax purposes.

How are my donations used?

+

Your donations support education, healthcare, housing, and livelihood programs for single mothers. We ensure 90% of funds directly benefit the families we serve, with 10% for operational costs.

Can I donate anonymously?

+

Yes, you can make an anonymous donation. During the checkout process, you can choose not to provide your name or contact information while still completing your donation securely.

Do you accept corporate sponsorships?

+

We welcome corporate partnerships and sponsorships. Please contact our corporate relations team for customized sponsorship packages and partnership opportunities.
