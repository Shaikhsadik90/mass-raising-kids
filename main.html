How We Help

Our comprehensive support process

1

Application & Assessment

Families apply through our program. We conduct thorough assessment of the family's needs and background.

2

Character & Audit Check

Our team conducts regular character and conduct audits. We ensure the mother and children are on the right path.

3

Education Monitoring

We track child's academic performance and provide tutoring support. Education is our top priority.

4

Ongoing Support

Regular follow-ups, financial assistance, and community mentorship throughout their journey with us.
