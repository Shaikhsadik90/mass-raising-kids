We assist children whose fathers are deceased due to death, divorce, or abandonment. Providing education, mentorship, and emotional support to help them thrive.

Our Programs
Who We Are

Filling the Void with
Community & Care.

Losing a father figure creates a gap that extends beyond just financial needs—it impacts emotional stability, guidance, and future security. Mass Raising Kids steps in to bridge that gap.

Based on data gathered by Waleed, Alex, and our dedicated team, we have identified key areas where these children need the most support. From our outreach in Scarborough to the wider community, we are here to help.

Toronto Outreach

Active community engagement and local support networks.

Data-Driven Approach

Strategies informed by comprehensive research from our team.
